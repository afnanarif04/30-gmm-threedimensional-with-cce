**Data Availability Statement**

The replication code and the processed empirical dataset used in this
study are publicly available at [REPOSITORY URL]. The repository contains
the Monte Carlo simulation code, the empirical estimation code, and the
processed bilateral trade panel assembled from publicly available sources.
Raw bilateral trade data were obtained from the CEPII BACI database
(https://www.cepii.fr); GDP data were obtained from the World Bank World
Development Indicators (series NY.GDP.MKTP.CD,
https://databank.worldbank.org) and the IMF World Economic Outlook
(https://www.imf.org/external/datamapper). Raw BACI annual files are
not redistributed in this repository; download instructions are provided
in the repository documentation.
